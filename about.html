About us

D's Delights is a home owned business, run by Denise Peters. After making cakes from family and friends for over 20 years, Denise has finally expanded her service to others. Whilst also being an annointed Reverend for NTA Church group, she is a mother of 4, a loving wife, and a counsellor to many. She loves making cakes and is happy to finally share this joy with others all around the world.
